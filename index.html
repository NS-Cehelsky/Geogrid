How to play

Fill all 9 cells with countries satisfying both the row and column category.

You have 12 guesses total — wrong ones count too. Each country can only be used once.

Scoring: 5–100 pts per cell. Smaller, less obvious countries score more!

Hint: You get one hint per game. It highlights the hardest cell and suggests a high-scoring country for it.

At the end you can see your answers vs the best possible picks for each cell.
